Direct Bonus

  1. Eligibility: To qualify for the Direct Bonus, a user must be registered as an "Active Partner" on the platform and must maintain an "active status" as defined in the platform’s policies.
  2. Earnings Calculation: Active Partners are entitled to receive 7% of the "Business Volume" ("BV") generated.
  3. Payment: The Direct Bonus is payable daily, the day following the generation of BV from sales.
  4. Active Status: An Active Partner must meet the minimum criteria for maintaining active status in order to continue earning the Direct Bonus.

Fast Start Bonus

  1. Eligibility: An Active Partner qualifies for the Fast Start Bonus by (i) maintaining active status and (ii) recruiting five new Active Partners within 30 days of enrollment, each of whom must (a) purchase a minimum of 50 BV on their first order and (b) maintain active status.
  2. Bonus Calculation: Upon satisfying the above conditions, the Active Partner is entitled to receive 50% of their personal BV during the 30-day period.
  3. Payment: The Fast Start Bonus is payable daily during the 30-day period, starting the day following the generation of personal BV.
  4. One-Time Bonus: The Fast Start Bonus is only payable once and must be earned within the 30-day period following the Active Partner’s enrolment date.

Team Bonus / Binary Commission

  1. Eligibility: To qualify for the Team Bonus, an Active Partner must: (i) Recruit two other Active Partners, one for each of the “Right Leg” and “Left Leg”; (ii) Ensure that both the Right Leg and Left Leg maintain active status; (iii) Ensure that the total weekly Business Volume ("Total Volume" or "TV") from both legs combined is at least 600 BV.
  2. Volume Ratio Requirement: The volume between the Right Leg and Left Leg must maintain a 2:1 ratio, where the leg generating more volume is referred to as the "Power Leg" and the leg generating less volume is the "Pay Leg".
  3. Bonus Calculation: Active Partners are entitled to 10% of the Pay Leg Volume. Bonuses are paid in increments of 100 BV.
  4. Weekly Payment Schedule: TV is calculated on a weekly basis, starting from Monday at 12:00 AM and ending on Sunday at 11:59 PM. Bonus payments are processed weekly.
  5. Earnings Cap: The weekly earnings from the Team Bonus are capped based on the Active Partner’s rank, as follows:
  • Iron, Silver, Gold: $5,000 USDT per week
  • Pearl: $5,000 USDT per week
  • Sapphire: $8,000 USDT per week
  • Ruby: $10,000 USDT per week
  • Emerald: $12,000 USDT per week
  • Diamond: $15,000 USDT per week
  • Blue Diamond: $20,000 USDT per week
  • Green Diamond: $30,000 USDT per week
  • Black Diamond: $40,000 USDT per week
  • Crown Diamond: $50,000 USDT per week
  • Double Crown Diamond: $75,000 USDT per week
  • Ambassador: $150,000 USDT per week
  • Royal Ambassador: $250,000 USDT per week
  • G.O.A.T: $500,000 USDT per week
  • Legendary G.O.A.T: $1,000,000 USDT per week
  1. Volume Carry-Over: Any unused volume from the Power Leg that remains after payout will carry over to the next week.
  2. Volume Flush: Any additional volume in the Power Leg, which exceeds the weekly cap, does not carry over to the following week and will be flushed.
